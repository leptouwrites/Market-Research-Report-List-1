<p><strong>The Car Refrigerators Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Compressor Type Car Refrigerator</li><li>Electric Cooler Type Car Refrigerator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The car refrigerators market features primarily two types: compressor type and electric cooler type. Compressor type car refrigerators operate using a compressor to cool the interior, offering rapid cooling and efficient temperature maintenance, akin to household refrigerators. In contrast, electric cooler type car refrigerators utilize thermoelectric technology, relying on the Peltier effect to reduce temperature without mechanical components, making them quieter and lighter but less efficient in extreme heat. Both serve distinct consumer needs based on portability and cooling performance.</p></p>
