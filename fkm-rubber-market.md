Low fluorine content FKM is suitable for less aggressive environments, offering cost-effectiveness and decent performance. In contrast, high fluorine content FKM provides superior chemical resistance and thermal stability, making it ideal for highly demanding applications in industries like automotive, aerospace, and pharmaceuticals. These distinctions help manufacturers choose the right material for specific needs.</p></p>
