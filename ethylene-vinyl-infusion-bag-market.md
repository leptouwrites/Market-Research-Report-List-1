<p><p>The Ethylene Vinyl Infusion Bag market comprises two main types: Single Chamber and Multi-Chamber bags. Single Chamber bags are designed for simple infusion applications, allowing for the mixing of a single solution efficiently. In contrast, Multi-Chamber bags facilitate the separation of multiple components until they are ready to be combined, enabling the mixing of different solutions at the point of use. This versatility caters to varying medical and pharmaceutical needs, enhancing safety and efficacy in drug delivery.</p></p>
