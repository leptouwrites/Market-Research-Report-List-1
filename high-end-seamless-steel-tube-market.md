<p><p>The high-end seamless steel tube market is primarily divided into two types: hot rolled seamless steel tubes and cold rolled seamless steel tubes. Hot rolled seamless tubes are produced at high temperatures, allowing for easier shaping and larger diameter production, making them suitable for heavy-duty applications. In contrast, cold rolled seamless tubes are processed at room temperature, offering tighter tolerances and superior surface finishes, making them ideal for precision engineering and high-strength applications. Both types cater to various industries, including automotive, oil and gas, and construction.</p></p>
