Single mechanical seals are commonly used in applications where leakage prevention is essential but not critical, offering ease of maintenance. Conversely, double mechanical seals are designed for more demanding environments, providing enhanced leakage protection and safety by containing any fluid. Both types ensure sanitary conditions in industries like food, pharmaceuticals, and biotechnology, where hygiene and reliability are paramount for operational efficiency.</p></p>
