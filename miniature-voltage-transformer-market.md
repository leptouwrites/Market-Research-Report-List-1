<p><p>The Miniature Voltage Transformer (MVT) market is segmented into two primary types: for measurement and for protection. Measurement MVTs are designed to accurately monitor voltage levels for metering and diagnostic purposes in electrical systems. They ensure precise performance data for grid management and energy consumption. On the other hand, protection MVTs are utilized in relay systems to safeguard electrical equipment from faults by providing accurate voltage information, enabling timely intervention to prevent system failures and enhance reliability.</p></p>
