<p><p>The wall power supply market encompasses various configurations tailored for different applications. Single output supplies deliver power through one output channel, ideal for straightforward devices. Dual tracking output supplies provide two outputs that adjust simultaneously, suitable for devices requiring complementary power levels. Dual isolated outputs offer two separate outputs with electrical isolation, enhancing safety. Triple isolated outputs further extend this by providing three isolated outputs, catering to complex systems. Other variants may include programmable or modular designs, addressing diverse user needs.</p></p>
