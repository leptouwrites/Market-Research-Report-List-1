<p><ul><li>Manual Quarter-Turn Actuator</li><li>Manual Multi-Turn Actuator</li><li>Manual Part-Turn Actuator</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The manual rotary actuator market comprises several types tailored to specific applications. Manual quarter-turn actuators are designed for valves that require a 90-degree rotation, ideal for on-off control. Manual multi-turn actuators facilitate multiple turns, suitable for applications needing precise positioning, such as sluice gates. Manual part-turn actuators serve devices that require a rotation of less than a quarter-turn, often used in butterfly valves. Additionally, the "others" category includes specialized actuators for unique or hybrid applications.</p></p>
