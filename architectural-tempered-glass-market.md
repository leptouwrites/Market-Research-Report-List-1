<p><ul><li>Flat Tempered Glass</li><li>Bent Tempered Glass</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The architectural tempered glass market is categorized into two main types: flat tempered glass and bent tempered glass. Flat tempered glass is produced through a heating and cooling process, resulting in a robust, durable product commonly used in facades, windows, and doors. Bent tempered glass, on the other hand, undergoes similar processes but is shaped into curves, making it ideal for applications like skylights, partitions, and custom architectural designs. Both types enhance safety and aesthetics in modern building structures.</p></p>
