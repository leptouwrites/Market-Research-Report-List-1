<p><p>The thermophotovoltaic (TPV) cells market is categorized into two main types: epitaxial method cells and non-epitaxial method cells. Epitaxial method cells are fabricated using high-quality crystal layers, allowing for improved efficiency and performance, suitable for high-temperature applications. In contrast, non-epitaxial method cells are typically less expensive to produce and offer flexibility in material use, though they may have lower efficiency. Both types are essential for harnessing thermal radiation for energy conversion in various applications, including waste heat recovery and solar energy.</p></p>
