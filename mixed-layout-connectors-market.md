<p><p>Mixed layout connectors are essential components in electronic assemblies, facilitating connections between different types of circuits. The market is typically divided into two types: pin contact and socket contact. Pin contact connectors feature protruding pins that insert into corresponding sockets, providing a robust connection. In contrast, socket contact connectors have receptacles designed to receive pins from other components, offering flexibility in assembly. Both types play a critical role in ensuring efficient signal transmission and reliability in various applications across industries.</p></p>
