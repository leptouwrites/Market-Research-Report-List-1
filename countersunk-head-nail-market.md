Cross (Phillips) nails feature a cruciform indent, allowing for better grip and torque when driven, making them suitable for various applications including woodworking and construction. Suri-Wari (Flat) nails have a flat head designed for flush finishes, ideal for use in cabinetry and furniture where aesthetics matter. Both types cater to specific needs in the construction and woodworking industries, influencing demand and market segmentation.</p></p>
