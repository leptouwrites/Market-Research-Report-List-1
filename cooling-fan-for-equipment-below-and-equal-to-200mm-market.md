<p><p>The cooling fan market for equipment with a diameter of 200mm or less consists of various types, including centrifugal fans and axial fans. Centrifugal fans use rotational energy to move air, creating high pressure and moving air through ducts, making them suitable for restricted spaces. Axial fans, on the other hand, move air parallel to the axis of the fan, providing high airflow with lower pressure, ideal for cooling applications where space is limited. Other types may include small DC fans or specialty fans designed for specific equipment needs.</p></p>
