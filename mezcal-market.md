<p><strong>The Mezcal Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Joven</li><li>Reposado</li><li>Anejo</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The mezcal market is categorized into three main types: Joven, Reposado, and Añejo. Joven is unaged, showcasing the pure flavors of the agave, while Reposado is aged for a minimum of two months to one year in oak barrels, imparting subtle flavors and a smoother profile. Añejo is aged for over one year, resulting in a richer, more complex taste. Each type caters to different consumer preferences, influencing pricing, marketing strategies, and distribution methods within the mezcal industry.</p></p>
