<p><p>Phase Sequence Indicators (PSIs) are devices used to detect the sequence of electrical phases in three-phase systems, ensuring proper functioning of motors and equipment. The market for PSIs can be classified into two main types: static and rotating. Static Type PSIs utilize electronic components to indicate phase sequence without moving parts, offering reliability and compactness. In contrast, Rotating Type PSIs use mechanical systems with moving indicators to display phase sequence, typically providing a visual representation through physical rotation.</p></p>
