<p><strong>The Pectin Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>High Methoxyl (HM) Pectin</li><li>Low Methoxyl (LMC) Pectin</li><li>Amidated low Methoxyl (LMA) Pectin</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The pectin market is categorized into three main types: High Methoxyl (HM) Pectin, Low Methoxyl (LMC) Pectin, and Amidated Low Methoxyl (LMA) Pectin. HM Pectin requires high sugar content and is commonly used in jelly and jam production, offering a firm gel structure. LMC Pectin, which functions with lower sugar content, is ideal for reduced-sugar products and dairy applications. LMA Pectin, with its added amide groups, enhances gelling properties, allowing for versatility in low-sugar and high-acid formulations.</p></p>
