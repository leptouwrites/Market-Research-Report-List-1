<p><strong>The Quantum Dot Display (QLED) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>QDEF</li><li>QLED</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Quantum Dot Display (QLED) utilizes nanometer-sized semiconductor particles to enhance color accuracy and energy efficiency in displays. The QLED market is segmented into two types: QDEF (Quantum Dot Enhancement Film) and traditional QLED. QDEF incorporates a layer of quantum dots to improve the performance of existing LCDs, enhancing brightness and color gamut without changing the panel. In contrast, true QLED displays combine quantum dots with LED backlighting to provide superior contrast and vibrant colors, delivering an immersive viewing experience.</p></p>
