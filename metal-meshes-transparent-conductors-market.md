<p><p>The transparent conductor market is primarily categorized into copper and silver metal meshes. Copper transparent conductors (TCs) offer advantages such as lower cost and effective conductivity but face challenges like oxidation and lower transparency compared to silver. Silver TCs are prized for their superior conductivity and optical clarity but come at a higher price. Both types are utilized in applications like touch screens, solar cells, and displays, with the choice between them often depending on specific performance and budget requirements.</p></p>
