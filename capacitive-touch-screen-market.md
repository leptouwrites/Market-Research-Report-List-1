<p><p>A capacitive touch screen is a type of display technology that detects touch input through the electrical properties of the human body. Unlike resistive touch screens, which rely on pressure, capacitive screens sense the change in capacitance when a finger touches the surface, allowing for more responsive and accurate interactions. This technology is widely used in smartphones, tablets, and other touchscreen devices due to its superior sensitivity, clarity, and durability.</p><p>The capacitive touch screen market is experiencing significant growth driven by increased demand for enhanced user interfaces across various sectors, including consumer electronics, automotive, and healthcare.
