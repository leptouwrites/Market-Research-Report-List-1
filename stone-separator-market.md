<p><strong>The Stone Separator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Trailed</li><li>Rotary</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The stone separator market encompasses two primary types: trailed and rotary separators. Trailed stone separators are towed behind a tractor, utilizing various mechanisms to lift and remove stones from the soil, making them effective for large-scale farming. Rotary stone separators, on the other hand, use rotating drums or discs to sift through soil, efficiently separating stones while minimizing soil disturbance. Both types are essential for enhancing soil quality and improving crop yields by reducing stone-related obstacles in agricultural fields.</p></p>
