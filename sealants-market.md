<p><strong>The Sealants Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Water-borne</li><li>Solvent-borne</li><li>Reactive</li><li>Hot-melts</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The sealants market is categorized into four main types: </p><p>1. **Water-borne** sealants, which utilize water as a solvent, are eco-friendly and low in volatile organic compounds (VOCs). </p><p>2. **Solvent-borne** sealants contain organic solvents, offering strong adhesion and flexibility, suitable for various applications.</p><p>3. **Reactive** sealants cure through chemical reactions, providing robust, durable bonds in challenging environments.</p><p>4. **Hot-melts** are thermoplastic sealants applied in a molten state, solidifying upon cooling, known for quick application and excellent adhesion. Each type serves specific needs across industries, including construction, automotive, and packaging.</p></p>
