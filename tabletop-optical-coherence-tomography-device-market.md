<p><ul><li>Spectral-Domain OCT</li><li>Swept-Source OCT</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Tabletop Optical Coherence Tomography (OCT) devices include two main market types: Spectral-Domain OCT (SD-OCT) and Swept-Source OCT (SS-OCT). SD-OCT employs a light source that captures multiple wavelengths simultaneously, allowing high-resolution imaging of tissue structures, primarily used in ophthalmology. In contrast, SS-OCT uses a tunable laser that sweeps through wavelengths, offering deeper imaging and better speed, making it suitable for various applications, including cardiology and dermatology. Both types enhance diagnostic capabilities in medical imaging.</p></p>
