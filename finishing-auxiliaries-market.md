<p><p>The finishing auxiliaries market is divided into two main types: organic finishing auxiliaries and inorganic finishing auxiliaries. Organic finishing auxiliaries are derived from natural or synthetic organic compounds and are primarily used to enhance fabric properties, such as softness and water repellency. In contrast, inorganic finishing auxiliaries consist of mineral-based compounds and are often utilized for effects like flame retardancy and anti-static properties. Both types play vital roles in textile finishing, improving performance and functionality of fabrics.</p></p>
