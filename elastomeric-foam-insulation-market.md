<p><p>The elastomeric foam insulation market is categorized into several types, including NBR (Nitrile Butadiene Rubber) based, EPDM (Ethylene Propylene Diene Monomer) based, and Chloroprene based foams, along with other specialized materials. Chloroprene foams provide strong chemical resistance and fire safety, while other materials may include innovative blends for specific performance needs.</p></p>
