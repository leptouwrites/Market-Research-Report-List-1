<p><p>The illuminated tactile switch market is divided into two primary types: surface-mounting and through-hole mounting. Surface-mounting switches are designed for direct attachment to the surface of a printed circuit board (PCB), offering a compact footprint and higher production efficiency. In contrast, through-hole mounting involves inserting the switch leads through holes in the PCB, providing greater stability and durability, particularly suited for high-reliability applications. Both types cater to diverse applications, including consumer electronics and automotive controls.</p></p>
