<p><strong>The Cellular-Connected Drone Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>BVLOS</li><li>VLOS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cellular-connected drone market is categorized into two primary types: BVLOS (Beyond Visual Line of Sight) and VLOS (Visual Line of Sight). VLOS drones operate within the pilot's line of sight, ensuring direct visual control during flights, suitable for applications like monitoring and small deliveries. BVLOS drones, on the other hand, operate at distances where the pilot cannot see them, leveraging cellular connectivity for communication and control, enabling broader applications such as infrastructure inspections and large-scale deliveries.</p></p>
