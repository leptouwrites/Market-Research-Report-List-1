<p><p>The organic fertilizers market comprises three main types: plant-based, animal-based, and mineral-based fertilizers. Plant-based fertilizers originate from decomposed plant materials, such as compost and green manure, enriching the soil with nutrients and improving its structure. Animal-based fertilizers, derived from livestock waste, provide essential nutrients like nitrogen and phosphorus. Mineral-based fertilizers, while still organic, come from natural mineral sources, ensuring a slow release of nutrients. Together, these types support sustainable agriculture and enhance soil health by promoting natural growth processes.</p></p>
