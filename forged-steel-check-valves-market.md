<p><p>Forged steel check valves are vital in controlling fluid flow in various applications, categorized primarily by their bonnet types: bolted bonnet, welded bonnet, and pressure seal bonnet. Bolted bonnet valves allow for easy maintenance and disassembly, making them popular for high-pressure environments. Welded bonnet valves provide enhanced leak resistance and structural integrity, suitable for critical applications. Pressure seal bonnet valves utilize a unique design that ensures tight sealing under high pressure, ideal for extreme conditions. Each type offers specific advantages for diverse industrial needs.</p></p>
