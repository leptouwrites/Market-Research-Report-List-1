<p><p>The solar cell junction box market is divided into two main types: potting and non-potting junction boxes. Potting solar cell junction boxes are filled with resin to protect electrical connections from moisture, dust, and mechanical stress, ensuring enhanced durability and reliability in harsh environments. In contrast, non-potting solar cell junction boxes do not use resin, which may allow for easier maintenance and repairs but can be less resistant to environmental factors. Both types cater to different installation needs and operating conditions.</p></p>
