<p><p>The antifriction bearings market is primarily segmented into ball bearings and roller bearings. Ball bearings use spherical balls to reduce friction between surfaces, providing high-speed performance and versatility across various applications. Roller bearings, on the other hand, utilize cylindrical rollers, offering greater load capacity and stability, making them suitable for heavy-duty applications. Both types are crucial in industries such as automotive, aerospace, and manufacturing, facilitating smoother movement, reducing wear, and enhancing overall operational efficiency.</p></p>
