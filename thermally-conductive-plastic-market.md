<p><p>The thermally conductive plastic market includes various types like Polyphenylene Sulfide (PPS), Polybutylene Terephthalate (PBT), Polyamide (PA), Polycarbonate (PC), Polyether Imide (PEI), and Polysulfone (PSU). Each type offers distinct thermal properties, making them suitable for diverse applications. PPS and PBT are known for their excellent thermal and chemical resistance, while PA provides good mechanical strength. PC offers high impact resistance, while PEI and PSU are recognized for their superior thermal stability, making them ideal for demanding electronic and automotive industries.</p></p>
