<p><ul><li>Extrusion</li><li>Calendering</li><li>Blown Film</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The LDPE geomembrane market features three primary types of manufacturing processes: extrusion, calendering, and blown film. </p><p>Extrusion involves melting LDPE pellets and forming sheets through a die, providing a seamless product ideal for large applications. </p><p>Calendering utilizes heated rollers to create thin, flat sheets, allowing for precise thickness control suitable for various projects. </p><p>Blown film technique produces tubular membranes by forcing molten LDPE through a die, offering flexibility and durability, commonly used in containment and environmental applications.</p></p>
