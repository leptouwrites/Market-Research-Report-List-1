<p><p>Agricultural utility adjuvants enhance the effectiveness of pesticides and herbicides. Compatibility agents ensure stable formulations by preventing ingredient separation. Buffers/acidifiers adjust pH levels for optimal product performance. Antifoam agents reduce unwanted foam during mixing and application. Water conditioners improve water quality, mitigating minerals that hinder efficacy. Antidrift agents minimize the drift of agrochemicals during application, ensuring precise targeting. Other utility adjuvants include a variety of specialized agents that support specific agricultural needs, improving overall crop management.</p></p>
