<p><p>The residential smart glass market features three main types. Electrochromic glass changes opacity when an electrical current is applied, providing privacy and energy efficiency. Thermochromic glass reacts to temperature changes, becoming opaque in response to heat, which helps regulate indoor climate. Photochromic glass automatically adjusts its tint based on sunlight exposure, reducing glare and enhancing comfort. Together, these technologies offer innovative solutions for energy savings, aesthetic appeal, and improved climate control in residential spaces.</p></p>
